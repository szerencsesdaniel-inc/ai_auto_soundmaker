8 Food and Drink
8.2 – At the Market: Buying Food
Level: A1

Characters:
• Lisa – customer, friendly and polite
• Seller – elderly lady, cheerful and helpful

Slide 1
Dialogue:
Lisa: Good morning.
Seller: Good morning. What would you like?
Lisa: I'd like some apples, please.
Seller: How many would you like?
Lisa: Five apples, please.

Slide 2
Dialogue:
Seller: Here you are. Anything else?
Lisa: Yes, I'd like some tomatoes.
Seller: These are very fresh. How many?
Lisa: Four tomatoes, please.

Slide 3
Dialogue:
Seller: That will be five euros.
Lisa: Here you are.
Seller: Thank you. Have a nice day!
Lisa: Thank you. Goodbye!
